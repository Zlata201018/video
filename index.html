Tutorial: How to Embed a YouTube Video

  1. Go to YouTube and find the video you want to embed on search panel. For example I look for Peppa Pig:

  2. Choose movie and click the Share button below the video.

  3. In the popup, click on Embed

  4. Copy the HTML code that appears

  5. Paste the copied code into your website's HTML where you want the video to appear

  6. Save your changes and refresh your website to see the embedded video
